### Phase 7 — Bug #5 discovered (IMAGE-KILLING)

While trying to run `VWBridgeTest suite run` via `POST /eval`, the listener wedged. Even the simplest probe — `(VWBridge singleton dispatch: 'GET /health HTTP/1.1' headers: (Dictionary new) body: '') copyFrom: 1 to: 80` — timed out. After that, TCP 127.0.0.1:9876 went into actively-refused state.

**Bug #5 (NEW, HIGH severity):** Recursive `bridge dispatch:` from inside `/eval` wedges the listener. Suspected mechanism: `/eval` runs on the bridge's serve process; calling `bridge dispatch:` on the same singleton re-enters the dispatcher synchronously, and some shared state on the singleton (or in the per-connection fork bookkeeping, or in the `onUIDo:` interrupt path) gets clobbered. The wedge propagated beyond the listener — manual click in MAS also stopped responding — so a **full VW image restart was required.**

**Bug #5 documented** in [`vw-bridge-known-issues.md`](./vw-bridge-known-issues.md#bug-5) with symptom, reproduction, root-cause hypothesis, workaround (don't call `bridge dispatch:` from `/eval`; run SUnit from a workspace OR refactor tests to call handler methods directly), recovery (image restart), and 2-stage proposed fix.
